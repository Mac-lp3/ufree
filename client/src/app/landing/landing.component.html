When you create an event, it is assigned a unique URL. Don't loose it.

Anyone with the URL can add information. Send it to your buddies so they can add their dates.

After 10 days of no activity, the event is automatically deleted.

Since anybody with the URL can see and add dates to your event, no personal info and try to keep the URL secret.
