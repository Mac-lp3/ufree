When you create an event, it is assigned a unique URL. Anyone with the URL can add information. Send it to your buddies so they can add their dates.

No passwords

Get the URL and go. After 10 days of no activity, the event is automatically deleted. Since anybody with the URL can see and add dates, try to keep the URL secret.
